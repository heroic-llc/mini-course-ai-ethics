# AI Ethics Fundamentals - Course Guide

## Course Context
This is a mini course for writingIO focused on ethical considerations when using AI.

## Video Script Guidelines
- Target length: 1-2 minutes per video (roughly 900 characters per minute)
- Use "A I" (with space) for proper text-to-speech pronunciation
- Keep language thoughtful but accessible
- Focus on practical ethical decision-making

## Lesson Topics
1. Why Ethics Matter - Introduction to AI ethics
2. Core Principles - Fundamental ethical guidelines
3. Bias and Fairness - Understanding AI bias
4. Transparency and Honesty - Importance of openness
5. Ethical Decisions - Practical ethical decision-making
