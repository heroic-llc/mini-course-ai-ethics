# AI Ethics Fundamentals

A mini course on the ethical considerations of using artificial intelligence.

## Course Overview

This course explores why ethics matter in AI, core ethical principles, issues of bias and fairness, the importance of transparency, and how to make ethical decisions when using AI.

## Lessons

1. **Why Ethics Matter** - The importance of ethics in AI
2. **Core Principles** - Fundamental ethical guidelines for AI use
3. **Bias and Fairness** - Understanding and addressing AI bias
4. **Transparency and Honesty** - The role of openness in ethical AI
5. **Ethical Decisions** - Making good choices when using AI
